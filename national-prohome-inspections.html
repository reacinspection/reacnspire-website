How It Works

From booking to report in 4 simple steps

We make the process straightforward — so you can focus on what matters.

1
Schedule Online or by Phone
Contact us with your property address and preferred inspection date. We'll confirm availability and send a booking confirmation.
2
We Inspect the Property
Our certified inspector conducts a thorough, systematic walkthrough of every accessible area — typically 2–4 hours depending on property size.
3
Receive Your Report
Within 48 hours you receive a detailed, photo-documented inspection report highlighting findings, severity ratings, and recommended next steps.
4
We Answer Your Questions
We walk you through the report — in English or Spanish — and help you understand what's critical, what to negotiate, and what can wait.
